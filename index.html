What's this all ABOUT
EyeMyth was created in the Indian digital sub-culture scene in 2011 as a celebration of ‘Visual Music’. Today it is India's only Media Arts Festival - unique in its open search for emergent South Asian identity within the global patchwork of digital creativity.
